# TasteSell

A daily AI strategist for SEA e-commerce sellers. Each session produces one Shop Brief — so sellers can create trend-matched content and win algorithmic discovery from day one.

## Language

**Shop Brief**:
The complete morning deliverable for one product on one day — containing a Trend Signal, a Selling Plan, and a Content Bundle. Generated via five Brief Steps with cascading results.
_Avoid_: Daily Brief, Generate Brief, morning report

**Brief Step**:
One visible milestone in Shop Brief generation — Scanning trends, Picking selling style, Writing copy, Creating image, Making hook video. Each step reveals its output as it completes.
_Avoid_: API call, pipeline stage, loading state

**Trend Signal**:
What's winning in the seller's category right now — trending formats, hashtags, and why they're working.
_Avoid_: Trend radar, trend data, market intel

**Trend Source**:
How the Trend Signal was produced — either `live` (Agnes web search) or `cached` (curated seed data). Always visible to the Seller.
_Avoid_: Fallback mode, offline trends

**Selling Plan**:
How to sell this product today — a Selling Style, a Target Buyer, and Why This Works (the agent's explanation). Agnes picks all three automatically; the Seller sees the reasoning.
_Avoid_: Taste Strategy, tactic classifier, archetype detection

**Selling Style**:
The content angle for this product. One of: `top-pick` (best product claim), `key-ingredient` (lead with what it's made of), `life-change` (show the transformation).
_Avoid_: Taste tactic, best-thing, hero-ingredient, transformation story

**Target Buyer**:
Who the copy is written for. One of: `quality-focused` (cares about real quality), `status-seeker` (buying to signal arrival), `budget-prestige` (wants the logo at entry price), `luxury-buyer` (generationally wealthy — rarely relevant in MVP).
_Avoid_: Buyer archetype, cultural omnivore, petite bourgeoisie, aspiring consumer

**Why This Works**:
A short plain-language explanation of why Agnes chose this Selling Style and Target Buyer — shown to the Seller, not hidden.
_Avoid_: Reasoning, classification output, agent logic

**Content Bundle**:
The post-ready outputs derived from the Selling Plan — Hook Line, Script, Channel Copy for TikTok, Shopee, and WhatsApp, hero image, and Hook Video.
_Avoid_: Generated content, copy bundle, assets

**Channel Copy**:
The post-ready text for one sales channel — TikTok caption, Shopee listing (title + description), or WhatsApp caption. No auto-publish in MVP; Seller copies and posts manually.
_Avoid_: Platform output, listing text, social post

**Hook Line**:
The 3-second opener text — the first line the Seller says or shows on camera.
_Avoid_: Hook, opener, attention grabber

**Hook Video**:
A short (5–10 second) video clip generated by Agnes-Video — the opening hook only, not a full TikTok. The Seller films or posts the rest using the Script.
_Avoid_: Full video, TikTok video, product demo video

**Video Source**:
How the Hook Video was produced — either `live` (Agnes-Video API) or `cached` (pre-generated fallback). Always visible to the Seller.
_Avoid_: Fallback video, offline video

**Brief Status**:
Whether the Shop Brief finished successfully — `complete` (all required steps done; Hook Video may be cached) or `incomplete` (Selling Plan, copy, or image failed).
_Avoid_: Done, ready, error state

**Demo Brief**:
A pre-saved Shop Brief for Rina's vitamin C serum — used only when live generation fails during the hackathon demo.
_Avoid_: Backup brief, sample output, mock data

**Script**:
A 15-second filming guide with shot directions — what the Seller says and shows on camera after the Hook Video.
_Avoid_: Video script, content plan

**Seller**:
A phone-first merchant in SEA who sells in categories where taste narrative drives purchase — beauty, fashion, food — regardless of tenure.
_Avoid_: Merchant, creator, Rina (Rina is a demo instance, not the domain term)

**Product**:
The thing a Seller is trying to sell today — described by name, price, and category. Photo is optional.
_Avoid_: Listing, SKU, item

**Category**:
The product vertical — one of `skincare`, `fashion`, or `food`. Required field; scopes the Trend Signal and Selling Plan.
_Avoid_: Vertical, niche, industry

**Locale**:
The language and market context for a Shop Brief. Defaults to Indonesia / Bahasa Indonesia in the MVP.
_Avoid_: Region, market, country setting

**Seller Group**:
A shared chat where Sellers post Shop Briefs and note which Selling Styles performed well. Roadmap feature — MVP shows a static mockup only, not a live build.
_Avoid_: Community, WhatsApp group, social feature
